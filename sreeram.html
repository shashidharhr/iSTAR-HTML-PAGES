Sreeraman Vaidyanathan

Co-Founder and MD  

Sreeram is responsible for ISTAR's overall product strategy and implementation. A technologist and educationist at heart, he's constantly dreaming up ways to use technology in crazy ways to radically redefine the teaching-learning experience to drive tangible improvements in learning outcomes.

He has an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad and a Masters in Computer Science from the University of Colorado,Boulder.

In a previous life, Sreeram co-founded Brewhaha Entertainment, a startup in the retail leisure entertainment and hospitality space where he became obsessed with gaming. Prior to starting Brewhaha, Sreeram worked as a Program Manager for Microsoft Corporation in its Enterprise Migration unit.
