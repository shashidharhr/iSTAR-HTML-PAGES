    State Skill Mission

DDU

The DDU - GKY is the Government of India's flagship rural development scheme with a heavy emphasis on rural livelihood generation through skills training.

iSTAR operates skill development centers in various states across India under the program. We aim to improve access for students at the bottom of the pyramid and bridge the opportunity divide for meritorious students in rural areas.

NULM

The National Urban Livelihood Mission is the umbrella scheme for creating urban livelihoods for eligible beneficiaries.

iSTAR operates skill development centers in various cities across India under the program with a view to improving access for students at the bottom of the pyramid and bridging the opportunity divide for meritorious students in poor urban communities.

PMKVY

The Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana is a pioneering attempt to catalyze the skill development ecosystem in India by incentivizing students to acquire skills for better career outcomes. iSTAR facilitates PMKVY through its network of partner training centers across the country.

ASAP

Additional Skill Acquisition Program (ASAP) is a Govt. of Kerala initiative to improve the employability of college and school students.

iSTAR has delivered training across all the districts of Kerala to over 5000 students under the program.

State Skill Missions

iSTAR works with State Skill Missions across India to implement skilling initiatives to improve employability outcomes.
