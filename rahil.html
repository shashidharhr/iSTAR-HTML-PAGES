Rahil Rangwala

Program Officer - Michael & Susan Dell Foundation.  

Rahil is a program officer in the family economic stability team in India and primarily oversees the low income housing portfolio. He spent over seven years at Bridgewater Associates, a global macro hedge fund, before making the transition to the development sector. Rahil has a bachelor’s in computer science from the University of Texas in Austin and a master’s in public administration with a focus on international development from New York University.
