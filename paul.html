Paul Thomas

Head Of Human Resources  

Paul who Heads the HR team at iStar is an MBA in Human Resources and has been with major start-ups and MNC'c since inception to building and managing scale, processes and profitability.

Paul over 12 years has had the advantage to be part of three very successful start-ups. With FitKids Education,he headed the HR team that created a culture of excellence and innovation. FitKids grew from just 3 people to more than 700 people in 4 years. Previously he was with Decathlon Sports (The World’s largest Sports retailer) as their first HR Manager and Brand Manager. and was involved in opening their first few stores across India.

Early in his career Paul was with Hutchinson 3G in Mumbai as a Trainer and Process Transition Expert during the initial stages when they were just a 80+ strong team to becoming a 10000+ strong company. He helped set up the Pune center as a Transition Expert and was involved in business process migrations from the UK to Mumbai.

Paul is an avid Sports person and loves to travel.
