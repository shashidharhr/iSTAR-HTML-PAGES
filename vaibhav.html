Vaibhav Verma

Vice President - Technology  

Vaibhav Verma is an IIT-Mumbai graduate whose passion lies in creating technology solutions for difficult to solve problems. Undeterred by challenges, Vaibhav sees great opportunities in redefining the way we approach the e-learning content delivery experience. Vaibhav's role in iSTAR as VP, Technology, is to create a versatile tech platform to disseminate iSTAR's content as well as to create a strong skeleton on which iSTAR's digital content strategy can grow in the future. Vaibhav's strength lies in his ability to condense complex ideas into simple and easy to understand solutions that are elegant in design and cutting edge in their vision.

A web 2.0 and e-commerce architect with over 15 years of experience, Vaibhav has worked in different kinds of companies including startups like Minglebox, Indipepal etc. He has architected and developed large scale applications, and worked with focused teams, and understands business context. He is experienced in using technologies across the whole stack including hosting, application development, databases and UI. He has also worked with varied web-based application delivery media.
